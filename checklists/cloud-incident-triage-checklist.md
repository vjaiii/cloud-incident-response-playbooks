# Cloud Incident Triage Checklist

## Validation

- What triggered the alert
- Which account, project, or subscription is affected
- Which identity or workload is involved
- Whether the activity is confirmed malicious, suspicious, or unknown

## Scope

- Affected resources
- Potential data impact
- IAM impact
- Network impact
- Workload impact

## Evidence

- Audit logs
- Authentication records
- Resource configuration history
- Detection alerts
- Related ticket or timeline entries

## Response

- Contain
- Investigate
- Recover
- Document
- Follow up with preventive controls
